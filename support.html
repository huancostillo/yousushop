Technical Support

Welcome to our technical support page. If you encounter any issues while using our app, please refer to the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) below or contact us using the provided contact information.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How to register an account?

You can complete the registration by clicking the "Register" button on the app's homepage and following the instructions to enter your email address, phone number, and set a password.

2. What if I forget my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password," enter your registered email or phone number, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

3. How to update the app?

You can search for our app in the app store (such as the App Store or Google Play). If a new version is available, click the "Update" button to install it.

4. How to provide feedback or suggestions?

You can submit your questions or suggestions through the "Feedback" feature in the app, and we will process them and respond to you as soon as possible.
